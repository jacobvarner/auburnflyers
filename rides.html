Auburn Flyers Group Rides

These are the scheduled group rides around Auburn that the Flyers lead and participate in. We hope we have something for everyone and if you have any questions or want more details, reach out to us on our contact page.

Tuesday Morning Crew

What: A faster paced morning ride with a quick 20ish miles for the people that have to make it to work and early classes. Typically 18-20mph and back by 7:15am.

When: Tuesdays @ 6:00am.

Where: Toomer's Corner.

CAMP Evening Rides and Grill

What: An intermediate to skilled mountain bike ride with time to grill afterwards.

When: Tuesdays @ 5:45pm.

Where: Chewacala State Park.

Wednesday Night Worlds

What: A weekly race simulation ride. The ride is typically ~30 miles with a 12 mile race segment in the middle. Overall, the pace is usually 18-20mph.

When: Wednesdays @ 5:15pm.

Where: James Bros Bikes.

Thursday Morning Crew

What: A faster paced morning ride with a quick 20ish miles for the people that have to make it to work and early classes. Typically 18-20mph and back by 7:15am.

When: Thursday @ 6:00am.

Where: Toomer's Corner.

Check back for new rides and updated times and locations, especially at the beginning of new semesters when everyone's schedules change a little.
