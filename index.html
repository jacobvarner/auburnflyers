The Auburn Flyers are Auburn University's official cycling club. Promoting cycling as a sport and as a hobby.

The Flyers race mountain bike, cyclocross, and road in the Southeastern Collegiate Cycling Conference as a club team competing against other club teams and even varsity cycling programs around the Southeast. In addition to racing, the Flyers also aim to promote cycling as a hobby in and around Auburn. If you're interested in racing with us, riding with us, or just want to find out more, get in touch on our contact page. We hope to ride with you soon. War Eagle!
